Blog Design System

Irina Kramer

For my personal blog I wanted to use a design system. I started doing research on available systems such as Material design, Bootstrap, Fluent and others developed by large companies. All of them were amazingly detailed and beautiful, but were too dense for my goal. So I decided to build a very lightweight design system and learn in the process.

Here is a screenshot of the result.

This design system is a work in progress and I intend to add new features as needed. My main goal is reusability of elements, like stated by the DRY (Don't Reapeat Yourself) principle in programming:

Every piece of knowledge must have a single, unambiguous, authoritative representation within a system.
- Andy Hunt, The Pragmatic Programmer
